Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Driven by Purity

Founded in 2007, Natural Goodness has grown from a small family operation into a trusted name in premium coconut processing. Under the brand Kalpa Ruchi, we bring the wholesome goodness of the coconut palm to kitchens and industries worldwide.

Every product — from silky virgin coconut oil to fine desiccated powder — is the result of careful sourcing, traditional expertise, and rigorous modern quality controls.

How We Work

From Grove to Global Markets

A five-step journey that guarantees purity in every packet.

1
Sourcing

Hand-picked mature coconuts from certified, sustainable farms across South India.

2
Extraction

Kernels freshly grated or pressed using cold-process technology to preserve nutrients.

3
Processing

Controlled drying, filtering, and refining under hygienic, food-safe conditions.

4
Quality Control

Rigorous lab testing for purity, moisture, microbial count, and food safety standards.

5
Packaging

Sealed in food-grade, tamper-proof packaging with full traceability and labelling.
